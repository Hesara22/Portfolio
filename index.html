IoT Based Smart Beehive monitoring System

This technology measures the hive's temperature, humidity, weight, rainfall and sounds in real time. After that, the data is examined to forecast honey production, predict bee swarming, and keep an eye on the hive's health. It also detects CO2 gas and the intensity of the rainfall. The ultimate objective is to give beekeepers practical insights for better sustainable and productive hive management. Ecosystems and the sustainability of agriculture are seriously threatened by the global drop in bee numbers. Bee health and hive management are frequently hampered by the ineffective monitoring mechanisms found in traditional beekeeping techniques. So this paved the way to introduce an intelligent IoT-based beehive monitoring system designed specifically for the Sri Lankan environment in order to solve these problems. Real-time data on temperature, humidity, sound, weight, and hive activity are collected by the system through the integration of multiple sensors. This data is analyzed by sophisticated algorithms that provide health monitoring, forecasts of honey output, and insights into hive conditions. Notifications for optimal harvesting, recommendations for feeding depending on environmental parameters, and early detection of swarming behavior are among the key features of the system made.
